# 💇‍♂️ Hair Salon Data Analysis (2025)

## 📊 Project Overview
This project analyzes real business data from a hair salon, focusing on revenue performance, service categories, employee contribution, and customer inactivity.

The goal is to identify key business insights and opportunities to increase revenue, especially through customer retention and reactivation strategies.

---

## 📅 Analysis Period
01/01/2025 – 31/12/2025

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts)
- Data Cleaning & Transformation
- Basic Business Analytics

---

## 📂 Dataset Description
The dataset includes:
- Customer services
- Categories (Haircuts, Color, Styling, etc.)
- Employee performance
- Revenue data
- Customer inactivity tracking

---

## 📈 Key Analysis

### 1. Revenue by Category
- Identifies which service categories generate the most revenue
- Haircuts dominate total revenue contribution

### 2. Revenue by Employee
- Compares employee performance
- Highlights top-performing staff

### 3. Top 5 Categories
- Focus on the most profitable services
- Helps prioritize business strategy

### 4. Customer Inactivity Segments
- Segmentation based on inactivity duration:
  - 3–6 months
  - 6–12 months
  - 12–24 months
  - 24+ months

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- A large percentage of customers have not visited the salon for over 24 months
- Only a small portion of customers remain active
- Significant opportunity exists in customer reactivation
- The business shows strong customer acquisition but weak retention

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Implement targeted reactivation campaigns (SMS / offers)
- Focus on high-value inactive customers first
- Introduce loyalty programs to improve retention
- Monitor customer return rates over time

---

## 📌 Conclusion
This analysis reveals that improving customer retention and reactivation can significantly increase revenue without acquiring new customers.
