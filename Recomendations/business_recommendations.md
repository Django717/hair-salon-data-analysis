# 💡 Business Insights & Recommendations

## 📊 Key Insights
- 💇‍♂️ The business is haircut-driven (highest revenue category)
- ⚠️ Revenue is highly dependent on a few services (Men & Women Haircut)
- 👥 Employee performance varies across service categories

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

### 1. Focus on High-Revenue Services
- Increase availability (more appointment slots)
- Improve promotion of top-performing services
- Review pricing strategy

### 2. Reduce Dependency on Few Services
- Upsell additional services (e.g. treatments)
- Create combo offers
- Promote underperforming categories

### 3. Optimize Staff Allocation
- Assign appointments based on employee strengths
- Use performance data for scheduling and training

---

## 💼 Business Value

This project provides a tool that helps salons:
- Understand revenue drivers
- Identify dependency risks
- Evaluate employee performance
- Discover growth opportunities
